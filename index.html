Questions We Get Asked

Do I have to pay anything upfront? +

No. We build the store, design the assets, and set everything up at our own cost. We only make money when you make a sale.

Who handles customer support? +

We do. We act as the "Merchant of Record" via Fourthwall. If a fan needs a size exchange or a refund, we handle the ticket. You never have to reply to a support email.

Do I own the designs? +

Yes. All artwork we create for you belongs to you. If you decide to leave Full Circle Merch, you take your designs and your customer data with you.

Do I have approval rights? +

100%. Nothing goes live on your store without your click of approval. We use a simple portal where you can approve designs in seconds.
